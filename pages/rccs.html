Reedsport Community Charter School

Empowering students in grades 7–12 to lead, learn, and succeed

RCCS provides rigorous academics, strong career and technical education (CTE), and a culture of support to students in grades 7 through 12. Our teachers and staff are dedicated to personalized learning and preparing every student for graduation, college, and career readiness.
