Parent & Family Resources

Empowering families to support student success at every level

Reedsport School District values strong school-home partnerships. Below are resources designed to help families stay informed, involved, and supported.

Helpful Links

  • ParentVUE Portal – Monitor grades, attendance, and school communication
  • Instructional Calendar – Important academic dates and holidays
  • Immunization Requirements – What your child needs to attend school
  • Food Services – Breakfast and lunch menus, meal applications
  • Bus Routes – Find your child’s stop and schedule

Get Involved

  • Attend school events and parent-teacher conferences
  • Join the Parent-Teacher Organization (PTO)
  • Volunteer for classroom or extracurricular support

Need Assistance?

If your family needs help with school supplies, transportation, language access, or community referrals, please contact your child’s school office or call the district at (541) 271-3656.
