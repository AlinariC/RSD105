Free Student Tutoring Resources

Accessing quality tutoring can make a significant difference in a student's academic journey. Below are some free tutoring resources available to students:

Online Tutoring Platforms

Schoolhouse.world

Offers free online tutoring in math, SAT prep, and more, connecting students with volunteer tutors worldwide.

Learn To Be

Provides free, 1-on-1 online tutoring for underserved K-12 students, focusing on personalized learning experiences.

UPchieve

Offers free, 24/7 online tutoring and college counseling for high school students, covering subjects like math, science, and SAT prep.

Connect Me Tutoring

A nonprofit organization providing free virtual tutoring and mentoring services to K-8 students, aiming to bridge educational gaps.

Local Tutoring Resources

Reedsport School District Tutoring

Contact your school's counseling office to inquire about available tutoring programs and support services.

Public Libraries

Many libraries offer free tutoring sessions or homework help programs. Check with your local library for schedules and availability.

Community Centers

Organizations like the YMCA and Boys & Girls Clubs often provide tutoring and academic support programs. Reach out to local centers for more information.

Additional Educational Resources

Khan Academy

Offers a vast library of free online courses, lessons, and practice in various subjects for students of all ages.

Curriki

Provides free, peer-reviewed educational resources for K-12 teachers, students, and parents.

For more information on student resources, visit the Reedsport School District website.
