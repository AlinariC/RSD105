RCCS Athletics

Building leadership, teamwork, and school spirit through competitive sports

Reedsport Community Charter School offers a wide variety of athletic opportunities for students in grades 7–12. Our sports programs promote physical fitness, personal growth, discipline, and the positive values of teamwork and perseverance.

Fall Sports

  • Football
  • Volleyball
  • Cross Country

Winter Sports

  • Boys Basketball
  • Girls Basketball
  • Wrestling

Spring Sports

  • Baseball
  • Softball
  • Track and Field

E-Sports

RCCS proudly supports a competitive E-Sports team, offering students a chance to participate in structured online gaming leagues and tournaments. E-Sports encourages strategic thinking, communication, and team-building in a high-tech environment.

  • Compete in statewide and national high school leagues
  • Games include Rocket League, Super Smash Bros, and more
  • Available to both middle and high school students

For more information, contact the RCCS Athletic Department or E-Sports Club Advisor.

Athletic Forms & Requirements

To participate in RCCS Athletics, students must:

  • Have a current physical exam on file
  • Submit an athletics participation form
  • Maintain academic eligibility

Trap Shooting Team

Our competitive Trap Shooting Team represents RCCS at regional meets and tournaments. This program teaches firearm safety, precision, discipline, and focus through structured coaching and training. Equipment and safety gear are provided.

  • Available to high school students
  • Meets weekly for range practice
  • Competes locally and statewide

Elementary Archery Program

Highland Elementary is proud to offer an after-school Archery Team as part of its enrichment program. Students learn focus, coordination, and confidence while having fun in a safe and structured environment.

  • Grades 4–6 eligible to participate
  • NASP-certified instruction
  • All equipment provided
