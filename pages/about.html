About Reedsport School District

Reedsport School District is dedicated to serving students from Pre-K through 12th grade. We believe in fostering a safe, inclusive environment that encourages learning, leadership, and lifelong success.

Mission

Empowering individuals to reach their full potential and achieve their goals.

Vision

To be an inclusive community, with heart, empowering all who enter our doors.
