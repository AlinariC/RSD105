Food Services

Healthy meals served daily to support learning and wellness

Reedsport School District is proud to offer free breakfast and lunch to all students during the school year as part of the Community Eligibility Provision (CEP).

Special Dietary Needs

If your child has allergies or dietary restrictions, please submit a physician’s note to the school office and speak with the nutrition coordinator.

Summer Meal Program

Free summer meals are available for children ages 1–18 at various community sites. Visit SummerFoodOregon.org to find a location near you.

Questions? Contact our Food Services department at (541) 271-3656.
