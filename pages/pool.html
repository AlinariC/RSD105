Highland Pool

A community aquatic center operated by Reedsport School District

Highland Pool is a year-round indoor aquatic facility located on the campus of Highland Elementary School in Reedsport, Oregon. Operated by the school district, the pool offers swim lessons, lap swim, public swim, fitness programs, and facility rentals to the community.

Public Swim Hours

  • Lap Swim: Monday–Friday: 6:00 AM – 8:00 AM & 4:00 PM – 6:00 PM
  • Open Swim: Saturday: 12:00 PM – 3:00 PM
  • Family Swim: Friday: 6:00 PM – 8:00 PM
  • Swim Lessons: By registration

Swim Programs

  • Learn-to-Swim Classes (Levels 1–6)
  • Parent & Me Water Introduction
  • Adult Swim Lessons
  • Lap Swim & Water Walking
  • Aquatic Fitness Classes
  • Private Coaching & Swim Team Practice

Facility Rentals

Highland Pool is available for private rentals including birthday parties, team practices, and private events. Reservations must be made in advance, and lifeguard staffing is included with the rental fee.
