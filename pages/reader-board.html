RCCS Reader Board Request

Promote your event or message on the RCCS electronic sign

The RCCS reader board is available to display announcements for school events, student recognitions, community activities, and more. Messages must be appropriate, school-related or community-beneficial, and approved by the administration.

Submission Guidelines

  • Requests must be submitted at least 5 business days in advance
  • Messages may be edited for clarity or length
  • Length limit: 2 lines of 24 characters (including spaces)
  • Maximum display duration: 7 consecutive days

Download Request Form

Complete the request form and return it to the RCCS front office or email it to the principal.
