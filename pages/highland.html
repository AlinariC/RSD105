Highland Elementary School

Inspiring curiosity, compassion, and confidence in every student

Highland Elementary serves students from PreK through 6th grade in a safe, supportive, and inclusive learning environment. Our staff is committed to building strong academic foundations and nurturing the whole child.

Programs & Services

  • Title I Reading Support
  • Special Education Services
  • School Counseling
  • After-School Enrichment

Arrival & Dismissal Procedures

Bus riders enter through the east gate; walkers and drop-offs use the front entrance. Staff are stationed for morning greetings and safety. Pick-up begins at 2:45 PM.
