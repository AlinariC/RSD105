P-EBT Benefits

Helping families with grocery support when school meals are disrupted

The Pandemic Electronic Benefit Transfer (P-EBT) program provides grocery assistance to families whose children miss school meals due to COVID-related closures or absences.

Who Qualifies?

  • Students enrolled in a school that offers free meals to all students (CEP)
  • Students absent for COVID-related reasons during a qualifying period

Benefit Amount

Benefit amounts vary by state and depend on the number of qualifying days. For Oregon families, benefit details are posted by DHS and are distributed via the Oregon Trail Card.
