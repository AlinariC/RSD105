Surplus Items for Sale

Giving our equipment a second life while supporting the community

Reedsport School District periodically declares surplus equipment and furniture available for public sale. These items may include computers, desks, chairs, tools, and other instructional materials no longer needed by the district.

Current Listings

Items for sale are listed as they become available. All sales are final and sold “as-is.” Check back regularly for updates.

  • 27" iMac Computers (2015 models) – $100 each
  • Classroom rolling whiteboards – $25 each
  • Student desks and chairs – FREE (pickup required)

How to Purchase

Interested parties should contact the district office to arrange payment and pickup. Items are available on a first-come, first-served basis.

Contact: info@rsd105.org
Phone: (541) 271-3656

Donation Options

If you are a nonprofit organization, school, or community group interested in receiving surplus items via donation, please contact us to inquire about eligibility.
