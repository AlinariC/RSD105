Charter Advisory Committee

Supporting innovation and student-centered learning at RCCS

The Charter Advisory Committee is a volunteer group that advises the district on policies, performance, and direction of Reedsport Community Charter School. The committee meets quarterly and includes parents, educators, administrators, and community members.

Responsibilities

  • Provide feedback on charter implementation and renewal
  • Review academic performance and student achievement goals
  • Foster strong school-community relationships

Interested in joining? Please contact the district office or the RCCS principal.
