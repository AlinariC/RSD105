School Board Election Filing Guide

Interested in serving on the Reedsport School Board?

School board members are volunteers elected by the community to help shape district policy and guide the future of our schools.

Eligibility

  • Must be a registered voter in the district
  • Must have lived in the district for at least one year
  • Must not be an employee of the school district
