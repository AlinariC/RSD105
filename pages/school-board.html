About the Board

The Reedsport School District is governed by a five-member board committed to guiding the vision and strategic direction of the district. The board sets policy, oversees budgeting, and works closely with district leadership to ensure equitable educational opportunities for all students.

Meeting Information

The board meets monthly on the second Wednesday at 6:30 PM at the District Office (100 Ranch Road, Reedsport) or virtually via Microsoft Teams.

Public Comment

Comments may be submitted in writing or via email to the board secretary at stipton@rsd105.com. Comments received before 3:00 PM on the meeting day will be read aloud during the meeting. Others will be added to the minutes.

Superintendent Awards

Each month, students are honored for their academic achievement at board meetings. These recognitions are nominated by staff and presented before the board and community.
