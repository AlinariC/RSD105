Immunization Requirements

Ensuring the health and safety of all students and staff

Oregon law requires that all students enrolled in public schools be up-to-date on their immunizations unless a valid medical or nonmedical exemption is filed.

Required Vaccinations

  • DTaP (Diphtheria, Tetanus, Pertussis)
  • Polio
  • MMR (Measles, Mumps, Rubella)
  • Hepatitis A
  • Hepatitis B
  • Varicella (Chickenpox)

Exemptions

Families wishing to file a nonmedical exemption must complete an online education module at healthoregon.org/vaccineexemption and submit the certificate to the school office.
